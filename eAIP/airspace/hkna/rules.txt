===== HKNA Nairobi Airspace Rules =====

==== Separation ====


==== CPDLC ====

  * CPDLC is available within the HKNA FIR airspace through the HKNA Logon Address.

==== SELCAL ====

  * SELCAL is NOT available within the HKNA FIR airspace.

==== SLOP ====

  * SLOP is NOT possible within the HKNA FIR airspace.


==== VFR Rules ====

  * VFR flights shall not be operadored above FL150 without approval.
